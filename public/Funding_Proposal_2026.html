🎯 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Global Election Network (GEN) is Pakistan's leading independent election monitoring organization, specializing in comprehensive electoral oversight in Gilgit-Baltistan and Azad Kashmir. Since our inception, we have successfully monitored all major elections in these regions, establishing ourselves as a trusted source of real-time, verified election data.


For the June 2026 General Elections, GEN is seeking strategic partnerships to deploy our most ambitious monitoring operation yet. Our proven methodology combines technology-driven data collection, trained local observers, and extensive media partnerships to deliver unprecedented transparency and public confidence.


  • 100% election coverage in GB & Kashmir (2018, 2020, 2024)
  • 200 trained observers deployed across all constituencies
  • Real-time results portal with 100K+ unique visitors
  • Media partnerships with 12+ regional channels
  • Endorsed by civil society and political stakeholders

🎯 2026 ELECTION MONITORING PLAN

Scope

  • Region: Gilgit-Baltistan Legislative Assembly & Azad Kashmir Legislative Assembly
  • Timeline: June 2026 (Pre-election → Election Day → Post-election)
  • Scale: 24 constituencies (GB), 45 constituencies (AJK)
  • Observers: 200 trained personnel deployed strategically
  • Coverage: Key polling stations, all constituencies, real-time reporting

Methodology

1. Pre-Election Phase (60 Days)

  • Stakeholder consultations with Election Commission, political parties, civil society
  • Observer recruitment and training (comprehensive 3-day curriculum)
  • Media partnership agreements and content planning
  • Technology platform readiness and testing
  • Baseline voter awareness surveys

2. Election Day Operations

  • Observer Deployment: 200 trained observers across key polling stations
  • Mobile Reporting: Real-time data capture via GEN mobile app
  • Media Coordination: Live coverage partnerships with 12+ TV channels
  • Results Verification: Cross-checking with official EC announcements
  • Incident Reporting: Rapid response to irregularities

3. Post-Election Phase (30 Days)

  • Comprehensive analysis report
  • Dispute resolution support
  • Recommendations for electoral reform
  • Public dissemination through webinars and media

Technology Platform

GEN Live Portal – Real-time election dashboard

  • Live results by constituency
  • Voter turnout tracking
  • Party performance analytics
  • Geographic data visualization
  • API for media integration

Mobile Observer App

  • GPS-tagged reporting
  • Photo/video evidence upload
  • Secure data transmission
  • Offline capability for remote areas
