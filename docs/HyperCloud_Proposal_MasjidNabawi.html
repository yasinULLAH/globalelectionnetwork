Product Overview

STM32 + FreeRTOS + Wi-Fi

Smart Water Dispenser Monitoring System

An advanced IoT-enabled monitoring solution built around the STM32F103C8T6 microcontroller and FreeRTOS real-time operating system. The system consists of a compact stainless steel weighing platform (30×50 cm) that sits directly underneath the water cooler, with an embedded LCD screen on the front face. Integrated strain-gauge sensors inside the platform measure water weight in real time while a DHT11 sensor monitors ambient temperature and humidity.

Data is displayed locally on an LCD screen and simultaneously uploaded to the ThingsCloud platform via ESP8266 Wi-Fi using MQTT, enabling remote monitoring from any mobile device or PC — ideal for large-scale mosque operations.

Site Selection & Cleaning

Select a flat surface for the water dispenser. Clean the surface of debris and water stains, ensuring it is dry and free of protrusions.

2

Steel Platform Placement

Place the stainless steel weighing platform flat on the cleaned floor surface. Ensure it is level and stable. The LCD display panel faces forward for easy reading.

3

Device Placement

Carefully lower the water cooler onto the centre of the steel platform, ensuring all feet rest fully on the platform surface. The cooler weight is measured through the platform's integrated strain gauges.

4

Wi-Fi Connection & Commissioning

Turn on your designated Wi-Fi hotspot. The device connects automatically. After pairing, verify data collection is working on the app/dashboard.

5

Alert Threshold Configuration

Set custom water level alarm thresholds through the app. Alerts will be pushed to all registered APP and PC devices when thresholds are breached.
